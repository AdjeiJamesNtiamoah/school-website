A Journey of Discovery

Our rigorous and innovative academic program empowers students to think critically, collaborate effectively, and engage with the world as curious, compassionate learners.

Our Curriculum

We offer a comprehensive and challenging curriculum designed to meet the developmental needs of students at every age.

Lower School (K-5)

Our Lower School program focuses on building a strong foundation in literacy and numeracy while fostering creativity and a love of learning. Through hands-on projects and play-based exploration, students develop critical thinking skills and social-emotional intelligence.

Middle School (Grades 6-8)

The Middle School years are a time of tremendous growth. Our curriculum is designed to be both challenging and supportive, helping students navigate adolescence while developing greater independence and responsibility. We offer a wide range of subjects, including world languages and the arts.

Upper School (Grades 9-12)

Our Upper School prepares students for success in college and beyond. With a robust selection of Advanced Placement (AP) courses, elective options, and independent study opportunities, students can tailor their education to their passions and goals. Our dedicated college counseling team guides students through every step of the application process.
