How to Apply

Our admissions process is designed to be straightforward and personal. Follow the steps below to begin your journey.

1

Inquire & Visit

The first step is to get to know us. Fill out our inquiry form and schedule a campus tour to see our students and teachers in action.

2

Submit Application

Complete the online application and submit the required documents, including transcripts and teacher recommendations, by the deadline.

3

Student & Family Interview

We'll invite you and your child for a friendly interview. This helps us understand your family's goals and allows your child to ask questions.

4

Admissions Decision

Our admissions committee will review all applications holistically. Decision letters are sent out on a common notification date.

Tuition & Financial Aid

We are committed to making a Your School education accessible to families from a wide range of economic backgrounds. We offer a robust financial aid program.

2024-2025 Tuition

  • Grades 6-8: $17,500

Financial Aid

Financial aid is awarded based on a family's demonstrated need. We use a third-party service to assess applications. Please visit our Financial Aid page for more details and to apply.
