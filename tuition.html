Tuition & Financial Aid

We are committed to making a Your School education accessible. We offer a variety of payment plans and a robust financial aid program to support our families.

2024-2025 Tuition & Fees

Tuition includes all core academic instruction, technology fees, and most field trips. Additional fees may apply for certain extracurricular activities.

Grade Level Annual Tuition
Kindergarten - Grade 5 $15,000
Grades 6 - 8 $17,500
Grades 9 - 12 $20,000
  • New Student Fee: A one-time fee of $500 for all new students.
  • Technology Fee: $250 per year, included in tuition.
  • Activity Fee: $100 per year, covers participation in most clubs and sports.
  • Lunch Program: Optional, billed quarterly.

Investing in Your Child's Future

We believe that a family's finances should not be a barrier to an outstanding education. Our need-based financial aid program helps bridge the gap between the cost of tuition and a family's ability to pay.

How to Apply for Aid

Financial aid applications are processed through a third-party service, Clarity. We use their confidential analysis to determine a family's demonstrated need.
